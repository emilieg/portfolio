Wino911

(link: https://wino911.herokuapp.com/) Wino911 helps you find a place that serves wine fast. Directions are provided to guide you to the closest destination so you can quench your thirst. If you like the place you can save it to your favorites list. The local temperature display helps you decide whether you should drink red or white.

Technology: JavaScript, Node.js, ExpressJS, Postgres, Sequelize APIs: Google Places, Google Directions, Forecast.io (link: https://github.com/emilieg/Wino911)

What's That?

(link: http://www.hackathon.io/what-s-that2) An app that translates scientific ingredient language into simple terms. Users can scan food, cleaning, and personal products by barcode and get more details about the ingredients in the items that they consume. This app was a group collaboration and built at the AngelHack Hackathon Seattle. Team: Michael, Monte, Nate, Emilie and Keidra I worked on one of the APIs, AngularJS, user story and presented the product to the judges Technology: Express, Node.js, AngularJS, Firebase (link: https://github.com/emilieg/whatsinthis )
