An app that translates scientific ingredient language into simple terms. Scan food, cleaning, and personal products by barcode and get detailed information about the ingredients.

WINO911

Wino911 provides you with directions to the closest wine bar. A local temperature display helps you decide whether you should drink red or white. Save your favorite bar to a favorites list. This is a full stack node.js app that features user auth and full CRUD capability.

CAPPSTONE

Cappstone is an app that helps you track all activities related to your job search. Check for upcoming appointments, add contacts, and add notes about meetings.

This app was build in Ruby on Rails as a group initiative with 3 other developers in 4 days.

STUCK IN THE MUD

Stuck in the Mud is a dice game where two players compete for the highest score. They are only able to score points when their roll does not include any twos or fives.
